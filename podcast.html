♥Why podcasting?

There are plenty of ways to market your business.

Very few let someone spend thirty minutes with you every week.

That's why podcasting works.

It creates trust in a way social media can't. It gives you long-form content that can be repurposed everywhere else. It builds a library that keeps introducing people to your work months, and even years, after each episode goes live.

But only when it's built with intention.

Most podcasts start the same way.

Someone buys a microphone. Records a trailer. Uploads one episode. Then spends the next three months wondering what to talk about, how to stay consistent, and whether anyone is listening.

The problem isn't motivation.

The problem is they built a podcast instead of building a system. That's exactly what we'll build together.

♥How it compounds

One conversation, working for months.

Every episode sends a signal outward: it becomes content, content becomes visibility, visibility becomes trust, until the right clients come to you.

You're not chasing attention every week. You're building an asset that compounds.
