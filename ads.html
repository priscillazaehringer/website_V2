♥No sugarcoating

What ads can and can't do.

The honest version.

Ads can

  • Put your offer in front of the right people, consistently
  • Scale what's already working
  • Build your email list predictably
  • Create a steady flow of leads and sales conversations
  • Play multiple roles: awareness, nurturing, retargeting, conversion

Ads can't

  • Fix a broken offer
  • Create demand where none exists
  • Replace strong messaging
  • Deliver overnight success, no matter what the gurus promise

If your offer converts at 2% organically, ads can help you get more of that 2%. They usually can't magically turn it into 20%. I'd rather set honest expectations than overpromise.

♥Questions, answered

Before you book, the honest answers.

How much should I spend?+

Plan on a minimum of about $150/mo, roughly $5 a day, in ad spend. That goes to Meta, not to me, and it's enough to test, gather real data, and start seeing leads. Once something's clearly working, you can scale up whenever you're ready.

How long before I see results?+

Leads often start landing within the first week or two of launch. But the real goal is a stable, optimized system, and that takes the full eight weeks to build, test, and refine. I'd rather set honest expectations than promise overnight wins.

Can you use my existing lead magnet?+

Absolutely. If you already have a freebie or lead magnet that converts, we'll build the whole campaign around it, no need to start from scratch.

Can you build one for me?+

Yes. If you don't have a lead magnet yet, we can create one together as part of the build, so your ads have something genuinely worth signing up for.

Can I keep you on after launch?+

If you'd rather not run it yourself, ongoing management is available for $397/mo. Otherwise the full system is yours, along with everything you need to run it solo.

Do I need an email platform first?+

Yes, you'll want an active email platform (Kit, Mailchimp, Flodesk, and the like) so we can connect new leads to a welcome sequence. Not sure which to pick? I'm happy to point you in the right direction.

Will I own everything?+

Completely. The ad account, the lead form, the thank-you page, the sequences, the SOPs, all of it is yours to keep. That's the whole point of how I work.
