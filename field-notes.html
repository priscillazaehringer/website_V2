♥What it is

Marketing, minus the theory.

A behind-the-scenes look at the work, as it actually happens.

Most marketing advice comes from people who stopped doing the work years ago. This is the opposite. I'm in ad accounts, funnels, launches, and podcasts every single week — and Marketing Field Notes is where I share what's working, what's not, and what I'm changing my mind about right now.

Quick, honest episodes you can listen to on a coffee break and actually use the same day. No guru energy, no recycled hot takes. Just field notes from someone still in it.

If you run your own marketing, this is the show that makes you feel less alone in it.

♥What we'll get into

The stuff I'm actually thinking about.

  • 📣What's working in ads this month

    Real tests, real numbers, and the changes I'd make before you spend another dollar.

  • 🧲Funnels & launches, unfiltered

  • 🎤Content & podcasting that compounds
